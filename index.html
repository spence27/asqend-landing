Data trust

Built for sensitive operating context.

Asqend only works if the business can trust what it connects. Pilot scope, access, and retention are explicit before systems are connected.

No model training on customer data. Customer context is processed to provide the service, not to train third-party models.

Human access requires consent. Customer content is only reviewed for implementation, support, or debugging with explicit approval.

Export and deletion on request. Offboarding includes customer-controlled export and deletion paths, subject to security and contract requirements.

Security review ready. DPA-level commitments, tenant isolation, and a SOC 2 roadmap are handled during pilot scoping.
